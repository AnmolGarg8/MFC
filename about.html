Our Heritage & Mission

Strategic Consulting Meets Multi-Trade Execution

Named with pride after the pristine Minnekhada region in British Columbia, **Minnekhada Facilities Consulting** was established to address a persistent challenge faced by property managers and strata councils: the headache of coordinating separate contractors for paving, line painting, janitorial care, duct cleaning, and window washing.

We bridged this gap by creating an all-in-one facilities management model. We combine high-level operational consulting with experienced, fully-insured trade crews to protect property investments and keep buildings spotless inside and out.

Our Core Standards

Built on Four Uncompromising Pillars

WorkSafeBC Safety

Zero-compromise safety protocols. Every technician is trained in Fall Protection, WHMIS, and high-reach equipment operation.

Eco-Friendly Cleaning

We utilize EcoLogo-certified non-toxic solutions and water reclamation units to safeguard local BC ecosystems.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees or unexpected extras. We provide itemized quotes and upfront project timelines before any work begins.

Rapid 24/7 Dispatch

Emergency leaks, parking lot damage, or urgent janitorial requests are responded to within hours across Greater Vancouver.
